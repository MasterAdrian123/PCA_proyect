LAW OF INERTIA

Every body remains in its state of rest or of uniform rectilinear motion unless other bodies act on it.

FUNDAMENTAL PRINCIPLE OF DYNAMICS

The force acting on a body is directly proportional to its acceleration

ACTION->REACTION PRINCIPLE

When one body exerts a force on another, the latter exerts an equal and opposite force on the first.

EQUATION OF A FORMULA

a linear function is characterized by being directly proportional between the dependent variable (y) and independent (x) of a function y = mx + b, where m is the slope and b is the intercept with the dependent variable; if the function is not linear, the linearization method is performed, as an example y = Ax ^ 2 + Bx ^ 2 + C becomes z = Ax + B, where B is the intersection of the line with the z axis on the graph of z vs x, finally A being the slope and C as the initial value of y at x = 0
